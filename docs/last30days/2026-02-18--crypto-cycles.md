## Glossary / Key Concepts

- **Halving:** Event every ~4 years where Bitcoin block rewards are cut in half; reduces new supply entering circulation
- **4-year cycle:** Historical pattern of post-halving bull runs followed by multi-year bear markets
- **Supercycle:** Theory that institutional adoption creates perpetual bull market without traditional bear phases
- **Supply shock:** When new supply is suddenly reduced (halving) but demand remains/grows, causing price to rise
- **Macro policy:** Federal Reserve interest rates, quantitative easing/tightening, dollar strength
- **RSI (Relative Strength Index):** Momentum indicator; >70 = overbought, <30 = oversold
- **ETF flows:** Institutional money entering/exiting Bitcoin through spot ETFs (e.g., BlackRock's IBIT)
